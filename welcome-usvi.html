Introduction

The United States Virgin Islands is made up of three islands: St. Thomas, St. John, and St. Croix . The most common language spoken is English . The Virgin Islands is a territoty of the United States. The three islands are a popular vacation destination due to being a close escape from the mainland US. Could it get more convienent? Of course it can, no passport needed! Before you book remember to bring

  1. Suncreen
  2. Sunglasses
  3. The whole family ( optional )
